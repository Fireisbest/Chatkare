      Festival Favourites

      Jau Sattu ka Laddu

      Take A Kadhai And Add Ghee , Add Jau Ka Sattu , Mix And Roast It , When Mixed Take Out In A Bowl , Add Jaggery In Kadhai And Melt It , Add Some Water , Melt It , Take The Roasted Sattu In A Bowl And Add Dry Fruits , Add Dry Ginger Powder , Add The Melted Jaggery , Mix Well , And Balls Of It , Serve With Dry Fruits Of Your Choice. Now Its Ready 😋

      ₹ 150

      Besan ka Laddu

      Add Desi Ghee In Kadhai , And Now Add Besan , Now Fry In Low Flame , Wait Some Mins But Don't Forget To Mix It Continuously Or It Will Get Burned , Now In Some Mins It Will Lose The Ghee , Whenever It Lose Its Ghee So Mix It Some Mins More And If You Think Besan Is Ready So Add Some Drops Of Water And Mix After Some Mins Turn Off The Gas And Leave It To Become Cold And Take Your Bura Suger And The Besan And Mix It Together And Then Make Balls Of It with soft hands. Your Laddus are now ready!

      ₹ 200

      Desserts

      Marble cake

      Powdered Suger , Milk , Vanilla Essence , Vinegar , Melted Butter , Mix Together , Now The Mixture In A Bowl And Add Maida , Add Baking Soda , Add Baking Powder , Now Mix It Together Untill A Smooth Batter , Now Add Half Mixture In Another Bowl , Add Coco Powder In One Bowl Of Batter , Mix It , Grease Cake Mould With Oil , Make The Design For Cake As Shown ,When Design Is Done So Take Kadhai With Salt And Add Stand , Using A Toothpick Make Lines On Cake , Keep The Cake Tin The Kadhai , Now Cook The Cake For 30 Mins , Cover The Hole With Paper. The Cake is Ready Now!

      ₹ 100

      Snacks

      Popcorn

      Turn on the stove with Medium Flame, Put on a Cooker and add Refined Oil, Add Turmeric and Salt. Now add Corn and Mix it all together to cover the ingredients on the Corn. Cover the Cooker with it's Lid but with the rubber and whistle part removed. Enjoy the Popping Noises! When the Noises are over, Get the corn out in a bowl and add Salt, and then add Black Pepper Powder according to Taste, Mix it all and its Ready!

      ₹ 30
